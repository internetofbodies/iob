Internet of Bodies

Bodies Equipments

Bio Sensor

A biosensor is a device that detects and measures biological information, converting it into electrical signals for analysis.

Health Monitoring

Health monitoring refers to tracking vital signs and health metrics, like heart rate,
blood pressure, or glucose levels, to assess and manage a person's health.

Smart Watch

A smartwatch is a wearable device that combines a watch with features like fitness tracking, notifications, and apps.

Bodies Connected

Bluetooth

enables secure, wireless communication between wearable and
implantable devices, allowing real-time health data transmission and connectivity.

WI-FI

provides wireless internet connectivity, enabling devices to communicate and share data over networks.

Internet

connects devices and networks globally for communication and data exchange.
